Pablo Picasso


What do you know about Pablo Picasso? This is his full name. "Pablo Diego José Francisco de Paula Juan Nepomuceno María de los Remedios Cipriano de la Santísima Trinidad Ruiz y Picasso" His mane is 23 words long. It's after various saints and relatives as Spanish law. He is one of the most famous artists of the 20th century. He succeed early and became a rich artist. After he died, his family fought a legal battle. He had 2 wives, 5 partners and 4 kids in his life.

He was born in Spain. There are some interesting story. When he was born, the midwife thought he was stillborn, so she left him on a table to attend his mother. His uncle was in there too. He was a doctor. Doctors at that time used to smoke. When he saw Picasso lying there, he blew smoke into his face. To that Picasso immediately reacted and started to crying.

Picasso completed his painting at 9. He entered the art school at 13. His parents enrolled him in the Royal Academy of Spanish Arts. After half year, he thought there were no more things to learn at school, so he used to played hooky, and went to museum.
